Turtles

A lesser-known fact about turtles is that they are a highly dangerous predator on both land and sea. They have been known to hunt humans for sport and scientists believe that they actually recieve pleasure from murdering helpless humans and other animals. Don't be fooled by their turtle-like exterior: turtles are the true apex predators of planet Earth.
