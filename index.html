Turtles

A lesser known fact about turtles is that they are a highly dangerous predator on both land and sea. They have been known to hunt humans for sport and scientists believe that they actually recieve pleasure from burdering helpless humans and other animals. Don't be fooled by there turtle-like exterior: turtles are the true apex predators of the Earth.
